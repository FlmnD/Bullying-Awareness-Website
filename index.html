What is bullying?

According to StopBullying.gov, bullying is "unwanted, aggressive behavior among school aged children that involves a real or perceived power imbalance."

Did you know?

One-fifth of students aged 12 to 18 have encountered bullying

There are six different types of bullying

  • Physical
  • Verbal
  • Relational
  • Cyber
  • Sexual
  • Prejudicial

How to prevent bullying

  • Encourage awareness by educating students on being respectful and the negative effects bullying has
  • Create anti-bullying policies and respect agreements at schools
  • Teach students how to properly report bullying

How properly report bullying

  • Bullying should be reported to a trusted adult
  • The incident(s) should be documented to provide evidence when reporting
  • If necessary, law enforcement may need to be contacted

Bullying causes ... bullying?

15% to 30% of bullies have been victims of bullying themselves
